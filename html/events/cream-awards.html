The Cream Awards

10th November 2011, 7:00PM Park Plaza

The Cream Yorkshire Awards are all about celebrating and rewarding creativity in Yorkshire.

Open exclusively to those based in Yorkshire and The Humber, these awards provide the opportunity for those in this region to compete with others in their area.

The results of the nominations will be revealed at an event to take place on Thursday 10th November in Leeds.

The Details

 

Date: 10th November 2011
Time: 7:00PM to 11:30PM
Cost: Free (ticket required)
Where to get tickets: Cream Awards tickets
Links: Cream Awards website
Organised by: The Drum
