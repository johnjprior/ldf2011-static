CheckIn CheckOff

20th November 2011, 12:00PM

CheckIn CheckOff is Leeds’ biggest digital adventure. Teams will need to use their skills, imagination and smartphones to uncover six top secret locations, successfully complete creative tasks & arrive ready for the final challenge at destination six ...!

To compete, teams will need:

  • 4-5 members
  • To be digitally savvy
  • A smartphone
  • Access to Twitter, Foursquare, QR code apps etc. (more info on website – coming soon)
  • To have signed up for the event via the CheckIn CheckOff website

Teams will unlock locations and creative tasks via check-ins. Creative tasks must be completed to earn points. To win, a team must have full points & be voted ‘best’ following completion of the final task. Hints coming soon!

The Details

 

Date: 20th November 2011
Time: 12:00PM - 4:00PM
